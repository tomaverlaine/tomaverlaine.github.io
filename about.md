<p>Chinese Postpunk Anthology is a collection of texts on independent music scenes across China.</p>

<p>It brings together notes, translations, and fragments written over time, without attempting to form a complete or unified narrative.</p>

<p>Post-punk appears frequently, though it is not treated as a fixed category. Rather than defining a genre, the texts trace how music is made, circulated, and thought about under specific conditions.</p>

<p>The selection is subjective. What is included matters, but so does what is left out.</p>

<p>Texts are written in English, Japanese, and occasionally Chinese, and are meant to be read discontinuously.</p>
