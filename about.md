Chinese Postpunk Anthology is a collection of texts on independent music scenes across China.

It brings together notes, translations, and fragments written over time, without attempting to form a complete or unified narrative.

Post-punk appears frequently, though it is not treated as a fixed category. Rather than defining a genre, the texts trace how music is made, circulated, and thought about under specific conditions.

The selection is subjective. What is included matters, but so does what is left out.Texts are written in Japanese, English, and occasionally Chinese.

Texts are meant to be read discontinuously.
